- Galaxia-Classic - 

A top down shooter game inspired by Galaga/Galaxian Classic. 
Dodge and kill enemies. Fight bosses. How far you can get?

Notable features:
- Boss fights
- Power ups
- Levels
- Sprite animation
- Sound effects and Background Music
- High score (saved by game)

Daily log: https://docs.google.com/document/d/1QDs4WQLTYkCw0QOnlWGWTGNZZbysIgwrLAkAzEpfGpc/edit?usp=sharing

Controls:

Use W/A/S/D or ARROW KEYS (← ↑ → ↓) to move. 
Use SPACEBAR or LEFT-CLICK to shoot bullets.

Hitting an enemy gives you **+100** points.
Killing the boss gives you **+1000** points. 

Collecting the health pack restores your health completely.
